Chicken Shawarma (Middle Eastern)

This Chicken Shawarma recipe is going to knock your socks off!

Just a handful of every day spices makes an incredible Chicken Shawarma

marinade that infuses the chicken with exotic Middle Eastern flavours. The smell when this is cooking is insane!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Thigh
  • Pita
  • Toum
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Grill the chicken
  2. Pre-heat the pita
  3. Spread the toum on the pita
  4. Add the remaining products: Chicken, pickles, and tomato(optional)
