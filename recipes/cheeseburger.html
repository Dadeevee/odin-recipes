Cheeseburger - double or single

I know you know how to make a cheeseburger … but as it turns out, I have some tips you might find useful!

From the best beef to the best bun to the best cheese, today’s burger recipe also comes with your choice of burger sauces

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Toast the buns
  2. Smear with sauce of choice
  3. Lettuce → tomato → patty
  4. Gherkins → onion
  5. Sauce on lid
  6. Jam the lid on top then demolish your burger!
